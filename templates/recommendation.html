Improve Your Sleep Quality

You are advised to improve and regulate your sleeping pattern by improving your quality of sleep. Below are the recommendations that you can follow for 21 days till you convert these into lifestyle:

  • 1. Regulate your dietary patterns.
  • 2. Improve your eating habits. Make sure you are eating healthy.
  • 3. Do breathing exercises.
  • 4. Do a 20-minute walk in early morning bright light.
  • 5. Do meditation for at least 15 minutes on a daily basis.
  • 6. Keep your gadgets away.
  • 7. You are strictly recommended to reduce your screen time, especially during late evening until you sleep.
  • 8. Follow sleep hygiene regularly.
  • 9. Do prayer or chanting before sleeping.
  • 10. Avoid daytime napping.

Note: Consistency is key. Following these recommendations for 21 days can help you turn them into lifelong habits.
